### B1: Compound Effects
Small consistent actions beat big sporadic efforts. Progress comes from daily deposits, not heroic sprints.

**Evidence:** Sporadic motivation leads to burnout and guilt. Steady habits produce surprising results over time.
**Implications:** Design systems and habits that make small daily progress automatic. Trust the process over feelings.
**Confidence:** High
